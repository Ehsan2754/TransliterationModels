SentencePiece is tokenization method.
It utilises unsupervised learning methode to  tokenize text.
Moreover it can detokenize text  NN text generation systems.
In this method, tokeniser uses a big vocabulary which is specialized for our sentences. 
SentencePiece uses methods such as BPE or unigram language model beside training from raw sentences.
SentencePiece does not depend on language-specific pre/postprocessing.
